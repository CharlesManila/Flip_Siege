Quick rules

Open full rulebook for every edge case.

  • Goal: enemy castle 0 HP (any round).
  • Trick: siege plays monsters, defense plays towers. Led color = lead monster color.
  • Follow: play led color if you can (may-follow @ 68 HP). Siege alternates each trick; calamity rounds 2 & 4.
  • Calamity: last trick flips two reserve monsters (ranks add to Assault); both teams tower-up — you choose your defense card.
  • Damage: Assault − Block. Off-color → Stash (rank = value).
  • Round ends: anyone at 1 card, or max tricks → Armory (2 buys).
  • Cooldown: played cards skip next deal (max 10 resting per team).
  • Armory: green = instant · blue = next round · Permanent = game (round 4+).
  • Permanent color: pick before buying; ties Sigil/Mastery/etc. to that color.
